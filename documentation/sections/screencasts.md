## Screencasts

*   [A Sip of EvelentScript](http://evelentscript.codeschool.com/) is a [Code School Course](https://www.codeschool.com) which combines 6 screencasts with in-browser coding to make learning fun. The first level is free to try out.
*   [Meet EvelentScript](https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/meet-evelentscript) is a 75-minute long screencast by PeepCode, now [PluralSight](https://www.pluralsight.com/). Highly memorable for its animations which demonstrate transforming EvelentScript into the equivalent JS.
*   If you’re looking for less of a time commitment, RailsCasts’ [EvelentScript Basics](http://railscasts.com/episodes/267-evelentscript-basics) should have you covered, hitting all of the important notes about EvelentScript in 11 minutes.
